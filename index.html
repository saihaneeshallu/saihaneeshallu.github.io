I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at The University of Texas at Dallas. Currently, I am advised by Dr. Yu Xiang in the Intelligent Robotics and Vision Lab. My research focuses on developing algorithms for autonomous navigation, semantic mapping, and learning from human demonstrations, realized through a mobile manipulator system. I aim to advance robotic systems that can effectively navigate and interact in complex environments to support human activities.

Before beginning my Ph.D., I co-founded VECROS Technologies , where I led a team in developing GPS-denied navigation and Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) capabilities for multi-rotor aircraft.
